Philosophy

I believe that software development is about turning complex requirements into clear, working solutions. I approach problems step by step, focusing first on understanding the logic and data structure behind a feature before writing code. Through my academic and capstone projects, I have developed a strong interest in backend systems, database design, and building reliable APIs that support real-world applications. I value clean structure, organized code, and teamwork, because well-designed systems depend on both technical clarity and strong collaboration. My career goal is to grow as a backend and full-stack developer by contributing to projects that require thoughtful system design and dependable functionality. In the long term, I aim to deepen my expertise in advanced software systems and intelligent technologies, while maintaining a disciplined and practical approach to development.
