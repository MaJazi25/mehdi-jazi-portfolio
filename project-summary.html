Project Summary

Contractor Connect is a web-based platform designed to connect customers with local contractors for home improvement and small construction projects. The goal of the system is to make it easier for customers to find reliable contractors while helping skilled workers access more job opportunities.

Many customers struggle to estimate project costs and often spend a lot of time contacting different contractors to compare prices. At the same time, many contractors face difficulties finding consistent work. Contractor Connect solves these problems by creating an online marketplace where customers can post projects and contractors can submit bids for the work.

The platform allows customers to describe their project, review contractor bids, and select the best contractor based on price, experience, and reviews. Contractors can search for available projects, submit proposals, and update the status of their work. The system also includes dashboards for users and administrators to manage projects and monitor activity.

By improving communication between customers and contractors, Contractor Connect helps make project planning faster, more transparent, and more efficient.
